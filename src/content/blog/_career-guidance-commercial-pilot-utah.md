Completing **commercial pilot training** is only the first step toward a rewarding aviation career. At **Blitz Aviation**, we provide guidance to help pilots in Utah explore career paths and find opportunities that match their skills and goals.

## Career Opportunities

- **Regional and Major Airlines:** Fly scheduled routes, build hours, and gain experience toward airline transport certification
- **Charter Services:** Flexible flying schedules, diverse routes, and exposure to corporate clients
- **Flight Instruction:** Become a CFI to gain experience, build hours, and mentor new pilots
- **Corporate Aviation:** Fly private company aircraft and manage business travel operations

## Building a Career

- **Networking:** Attend aviation events, join pilot associations, and connect with local flight schools
- **Logbook Management:** Maintain accurate records of flight hours and ratings
- **Additional Ratings:** Multi-engine, instrument, and type ratings enhance employability
- **Resume & Interview Prep:** Tailored guidance from Blitz Aviation instructors with industry insight
