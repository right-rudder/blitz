Becoming a **Certified Flight Instructor (CFI)** is a rewarding way to advance your aviation career, share your passion for flying, and gain valuable experience. At **Blitz Aviation**, we offer a structured CFI program designed to prepare you for teaching others while sharpening your own skills.

## What Is a CFI?

A **Certified Flight Instructor** is a pilot who is authorized by the FAA to teach students to fly. CFIs play a crucial role in the aviation community by:

- Training student pilots toward their **Private Pilot License (PPL)**
- Instructing advanced ratings, including **Instrument (IFR)** and **Commercial Pilot (CPL)** training
- Helping pilots build flight hours and experience for career advancement

## Eligibility Requirements for CFI Training

Before enrolling in a CFI program, you must:

- Hold a **Commercial Pilot License (CPL)** or higher
- Possess an **Instrument Rating (IFR)**
- Meet FAA medical certificate requirements
- Complete a minimum number of flight hours as defined by the FAA
- Demonstrate strong flying skills and knowledge of aviation regulations

## Step-by-Step CFI Training in Utah

### Step 1: Pre-Course Assessment

- Meet with our chief flight instructor
- Review your flight experience and certifications
- Identify strengths and areas for growth

### Step 2: Ground Instruction

- Learn instructional techniques and teaching methodologies
- Master FAA knowledge requirements for CFI checkride
- Practice lesson planning and student evaluation

### Step 3: Flight Instruction

- Conduct dual flights with a mentor CFI
- Learn to demonstrate maneuvers and procedures effectively
- Build confidence in instructing diverse student pilots

### Step 4: CFI Checkride Preparation

- Review FAA-mandated maneuvers and knowledge tests
- Conduct mock flights with feedback from experienced instructors
- Focus on teaching clarity, safety, and professionalism

### Step 5: FAA CFI Checkride

- Complete the oral exam covering regulations, aerodynamics, and teaching principles
- Demonstrate flight instruction skills in the cockpit
- Obtain your **CFI certificate** upon successful completion
